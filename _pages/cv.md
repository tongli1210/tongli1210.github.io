Work Experience
======
* Dec. 2025 - Present: Assistant Professor
  * School of Biomedical Engineering, Shenzhen University, Shenzhen, China
  * Research Interest:  
      * Medical image processing
      * Machine learning and deep learning for biomedical and healthcare applications, especially focused in multi-modal biomedical data fusion for precision medicine
      * Large language models and multi-modal vision lanauge models 

* Aug. 2021 - Nov. 2025: Staff Engineer (Medical Imaging Analysis/Machine Learning)
  * Shanghai United Imaging Healthcare Co., Ltd., Shanghai, China
  * Led a team of fifteen engineers as the technical leader (six Ph.D. and seven Master’s), driving AI-enpowered technology and application development for MRI systems.
  * Successfully developed products that are widely deployed across United Imaging’s full range of 1.5T, 3T, and 5T MRI devices, providing high-quality and efficient medical imaging services to a large number of patients worldwide.
  * Collaborated closely with multiple leading hospitals and research institutions, resulting in over 20 domestic and international patents.

* Aug. 2014 - Dec. 2020: Graduate Research Assistant
  * Georgia Institute of Technology and Emory University, Atlanta, GA, USA
  * Supervisor: Professor May D. Wang
  * Multi-Modality Biomedical Data Integration
    * Designed deep autoencoders to combine medical images (MRI & PET) with genomics and electronic health records (EHRs) for accurate diagnosis of Alzheimer’s Disease (AD).
    * Developed a cross-modality translation network to integrate multi-omics data for cancer survival analysis.
    * Designed a divergence-based consensus learning method to learn modality-invariant representation.
    * Proposed a gene embedding-based collaborative filtering framework for multi-omics data integration.
  * Medical Imaging Analysis
    * Developed a convolutional autoencoder for semi-supervised learning of endomicroscopic images for improved diagnosis of Barrett’s Esophagus (BE).
    * Designed a whole-slide imaging (WSI) classification pipeline for heart rejection diagnosis.
    * Developed a multi-scale convolutional network for improved classification by utilizing image pyramid of WSI.

* Summer 2018 & 2019: Summer Intern (Medical Imaging Analysis/Machine Learning)
  * Intuitive Surgical, Inc, Sunnyvale, CA, USA
  * Deep Learning based CT Images Segmentation for Lung Blood Vessels(May 2019 - Aug 2019)
    * Implemented a 3D U-Net model for volumetric medical image segmentation.
    * Developed adversarial training to regularize the network with Wasserstein distance or multi-scale L1 loss.
  * Partial 3D Reconstruction with Digital Tomosynthesis (DTS)(May 2018 - Aug 2018)
    * Implemented a pipeline for C-arm X-ray camera calibration and pose estimation with 2D fiducial markers.
    * Implemented partial 3D chest X-ray reconstruction with the shift-and-add (SAA) algorithm and the simultaneous algebraic reconstruction technique (SART).
